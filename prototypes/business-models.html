Software as a Service (SaaS)

Business Model
Description:

Subscription-based access to cloud-hosted software

Revenue Source:

Monthly/annual subscriptions

Examples:

Salesforce, Slack, HubSpot, Zoom

Key Metrics:

MRR, CAC, LTV, churn rate

Use Case:

Cloud software for business workflows

Platform/IaaS (PaaS/IaaS)

Business Model
Description:

Cloud infrastructure or platforms for developers

Revenue Source:

Usage-based pricing or subscriptions

Examples:

AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud

Key Metrics:

Usage rates, uptime, adoption

Use Case:

Cloud platforms for app deployment

Marketplace

Business Model
Description:

Connects buyers and sellers via a digital platform

Revenue Source:

Transaction fees, listing fees, commissions

Examples:

Amazon, Uber, Airbnb, Fiverr

Key Metrics:

GMV, take rate, liquidity ratio

Use Case:

Connecting buyers and sellers

Freemium

Business Model
Description:

Core product is free, advanced features are paid

Revenue Source:

Upgrades from free to paid plans

Examples:

Dropbox, Zoom, Notion

Key Metrics:

Conversion rate, retention, CAC

Use Case:

Free core product with paid upgrades
